About Us

Welcome to My News Portal. Our website shares the latest news, important updates, and trending stories from different categories such as politics, sports, entertainment, technology, and more.

This news portal is created as a simple collaborative project to provide users with a clean and easy way to read daily news. Our goal is to make information accessible, clear, and useful for everyone.

We are working as a team to design and develop this portal with basic web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
